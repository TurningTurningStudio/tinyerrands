about

The forest remembers everything you do, even the small things.

Tiny Errands follows Dusk, a small pixie moving through a living forest filled with supernatural inhabitants, old friends, and playful forest spirits.

It’s a short narrative game focused on atmosphere, characters, and dialogue. Nothing rushes you, but nothing stays still either.

gameplay

Simple tasks. Strange outcomes.

You wander between small forest spaces, meet odd characters, and complete errands that tend to change shape halfway through.

The mechanics stay light. The tone does not. Choices are subtle but persistent.
