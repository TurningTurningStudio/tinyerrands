About

Tiny Errands is a short, cozy RPG where you play as Dusk, a small forest spirit trying to complete simple errands that never stay simple. The world reacts to how you move through it—soft choices, strange encounters, and consequences that feel more emotional than mechanical.
Built to feel like walking through a half-remembered dream: warm, eerie, and gently falling apart at the edges. Expect cozy exploration, character-driven dialogue, and a world that shifts depending on your choices.

Gameplay

You explore a small forest map, meet strange inhabitants, and complete “errands” that rarely go as planned. Choices are light mechanically but heavy emotionally—what you ignore matters as much as what you do.
• Dialogue-driven exploration
• Light puzzle-like errands
• Branching character outcomes
• Short playtime, replayable paths
