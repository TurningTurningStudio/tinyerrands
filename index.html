about

Errands to cross off, and people to meet.

Tiny Errands follows Dusk, a small pixie moving through a living forest filled with supernatural inhabitants, old friends, and shy forest spirits.

It’s a short narrative game focused on atmosphere, characters, and dialogue. Nothing rushes you. The forest is happy to move at your pace. All you need to do is help Dusk find the perfect birthday gift for his human.

gameplay

Simple errands. A small curse.

Dusk needs help with a present, but his friends need help with a curse. You wander between small forest spaces, meet odd characters, and complete errands for your friends.

The mechanics are light. The game is peaceful. There are secrets to discover.
