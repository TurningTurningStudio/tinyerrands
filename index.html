About

You have a list of things to do. The forest has other plans.

Tiny Errands follows Dusk, a night pixie navigating a vibrant woodland filled with supernatural residents, playful spirits, old friends, and hidden mysteries.

What begins as a simple search for the perfect birthday gift slowly unfolds into a journey through the lives of the forest's inhabitants.

```
```
Gameplay

Wander. Explore. Help your friends.

Travel between handcrafted locations, meet memorable characters, uncover secrets, and complete small tasks that reveal a much larger world.

Tiny Errands focuses on dialogue, atmosphere, exploration, and character-driven storytelling.
